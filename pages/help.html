What is Join?

The "Join" homepage embraces the powerful Kanban method to optimize task organization and encourage effective team collaboration. Through its intuitive "Summary," "Board," "AddTask," and "Contacts" tabs, "Join" offers a user-friendly platform for managing workflows and driving project success.

This concise overview enables users to promptly assess the project's current status, aiding in better decision-making and resource allocation.
In the heart of the "Join" homepage lies the dynamic "Board" tab, boasting an interactive Kanban board. Users can effortlessly create tasks, assign priorities, and seamlessly move tasks between columns using Drag & Drop functionality. Real-time updates and visual cues keep teams informed, enabling quick responses to evolving project requirements.
The "AddTask" tab simplifies task incorporation into the workflow. In just a few clicks, users can input task details, set due dates, and assign responsible team members, ensuring comprehensive and accurate task management.
Moreover, the "Contacts" tab serves as a centralized repository of key team members and stakeholders. Displaying their names, email addresses, and phone numbers, this feature fosters seamless communication and collaboration, ensuring everyone stays connected throughout the project's lifecycle.

Designed with user-friendliness in mind, "Join" boasts an intuitive interface and logical layout, catering to users of all skill levels. This ensures quick adaptation to the platform, enhancing overall productivity in managing tasks and projects.

How to use it

  1. Registration or Login

    • If you are a new user, click on the "Register" button to create an account. Provide your email address, choose a username, and set a secure password. Confirm your registration by clicking the confirmation link sent to your email.
    • If you already have an account, click on the "Login" button. Enter your email address and password, then click "Login" to access the "Join" homepage.

  2. Exploring the Homepage

    • Summary Tab: Start on the "Summary" tab, which provides a quick overview of the tasks in each column (ToDo, In Progress, Awaiting Feedback, Done) of the Kanban board. This summary helps you grasp the project's current status at a glance.
    • Board Tab: Move to the "Board" tab, where you'll find the interactive Kanban board. Here, you can manage tasks effectively. To add a new task, click on the "Add Task" button in the respective column. Use Drag & Drop to move tasks between columns and update their status.
    • AddTask Tab: If you need to add a new task, navigate to the "AddTask" tab. Enter a clear task description, select the appropriate column (ToDo, In Progress, Awaiting Feedback), and set a due date if needed. Assign the task to a team member if applicable. Click "Save" to add the task to the Kanban board.
    • Contacts Tab: In the "Contacts" tab, you'll find a list of essential team members and stakeholders. Access their names, email addresses, and phone numbers, facilitating seamless communication and collaboration.

  3. Working with the Kanban Board

    • Task Management: In the "Board" tab, click on a task card to view its details. Edit task titles, descriptions, due dates, or assigned team members as necessary. Drag & Drop tasks between columns to update their progress.
    • Task Filtering: Use the "Board" tab's filtering options to focus on specific tasks. Filter tasks based on status, due date, or assigned team member to streamline your view and prioritize effectively.

  4. Collaboration and Communication

    • Share the Board: Collaborate with your team members by sharing the "Join" homepage. Provide access to relevant team members, enabling them to contribute and stay updated on project progress.
    • Contacts Management: In the "Contacts" tab, you can Add Contact to include new team members or stakeholders. Provide their names, email addresses, and phone numbers to keep essential contacts in one place.
    • Edit Contact: Update contact details as needed. Click on the contact card, make the necessary changes, and save the updated information to ensure accurate and up-to-date contact information.

  5. Stay Organized and Productive

    • Regularly Update the Board: Keep the Kanban board up-to-date to ensure accurate project tracking. Move tasks as they progress, add new tasks when needed, and update task details as necessary.
    • Monitor Progress: Regularly check the "Summary" tab to review the project's overall status and identify areas that may require attention or further action.
